Lasagna


Photo by sunorwind on Unsplash

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or cheese, and melted cheese, baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • 9 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 400g jarred pasta sauce
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Optional: salt, pepper, dried herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Assemble lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a layer of pasta sauce, then noodles, ricotta, mozzarella. Repeat for 2–3 layers, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top. Sprinkle Parmesan.
  3. Bake: Cover with foil and bake 25 min, then remove foil and bake 10–15 min until golden.
  4. Rest & serve: Let sit 5–10 minutes, then slice and enjoy.
