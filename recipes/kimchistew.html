Kimchi Stew (Kimchi Jjigae)


Photo by rawkkim on Unsplash

Description

A spicy and savory Korean stew made with fermented kimchi, pork or tofu, and vegetables, simmered to develop rich flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • 21 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • Optional: garlic, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Cook protein: In a pot, sauté pork or tofu with garlic and onion until slightly browned.
  2. Add kimchi & seasoning: Stir in kimchi, gochujang, soy sauce, and water/broth. Bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer: Reduce heat and simmer 15–20 min. Add green onion before serving.
